A little about me

Hi there , I'm Nurha. Entrepreneur and co-founder of Machinelf.

With a few years of programming under my belt, I decided to take the leap from freelancer to entrepreneur. I believe that starting your own business is one of the most empowering things you can do and an amazing learning experience.

I am really passionate about people and creating a space in which they can learn and thrive. As well as learning and growing as much as I can on personal and professional level. I have a special affinity for animals and keeping the environment clean.

I believe that awareness is the key to success - a tenet I live out through training, yoga and meditation. I am also very passionate about hiking and connecting to nature. Feel free to use the contact me section if you would like to connect.
